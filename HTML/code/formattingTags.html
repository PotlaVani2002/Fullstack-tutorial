Formatting Tags
Formatting tags are used to format the text. 
    1. b tag : to make something in bold format
    2. strong: make the text bold --user can't find any difference b and strong tags --search engines will give more importance to strong --The strong element represents strong importance, seriousness or urgency for its contents
    3. i tag: italic --sleepy letters
    4. em tag: emphasis ---make the text in italic form --in user perspective i and em tag has no difference ..but search engines will understand em better than i tag.
    5. u tag: underline
    6. strike tag:Strike is nothing but del tag
    7. del tag:it is similar to strike tag
    8. ins tag:del tag used to insert words
    9. sup tag:super script --maths formulas eg: a3
    10. sub tag: subscript --formulas chemical eg: H2
    11. q tag:  Quotation
